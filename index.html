The Augusta Developer

Who we are.

The Augusta Developer is a monthly networking and workshop meetup where creatives, thinkers and makers can come together, as a community to learn, mingle, and talk shop about the latest and greatest innovations in the ever-changing world of web development. Schuster Braun, the meetup's organizer, enthusiastically shared, "Hang out and discuss tips, tricks, and hacks with some of the best designers and developers around."
